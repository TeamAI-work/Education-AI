## Phase Objectives
Support specialized learning and future career planning through performance analytics.

## Working (Mechanism)
1. **Career Pathway**: AI analyzes performance across all subjects (strong/weak areas) to suggest career paths (e.g., Engineering, Medicine, Humanities) and necessary steps.
2. **Chapter Wise Weightage**: Interactive data visualizations (charts) that show which chapters hold the most marks in board exams, helping students prioritize.
3. **Integration**: All previous productivity tools (Note Taker, Concept Map, Mock Test, Pomodoro, Streaks) are available in this high-level dashboard.

## Need (Rationale)
11th and 12th-grade students are making critical career decisions. Providing a clear pathway based on their actual performance data removes ambiguity. Chapter weightage ensures they study "smart" by focusing on high-impact topics.
