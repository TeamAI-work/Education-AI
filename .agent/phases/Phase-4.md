## Phase Objectives
Provide high-stakes exam preparation tools and visual synthesis capabilities.

## Working (Mechanism)
1. **Concept Mapping**: Uses `react-flow` to visualize AI-generated relationships between topics in a dynamic node-link diagram.
2. **Mock Test Simulator**: 
   - AI generates open-ended questions based on NCERT standards.
   - User inputs answers (text).
   - AI grades based on exam marking schemes and provides feedback.
3. **Pomodoro Timer**: A productivity tool following the 25/5 minute cycle to optimize study blocks.

## Need (Rationale)
9th and 10th-grade students face their first major board exams. Mind mapping helps in visualizing complex scientific or social studies concepts. Mock tests reduce exam anxiety and provide realistic performance metrics.
