## Phase Objectives
Introduce study habits and information organization tools for middle school students.

## Working (Mechanism)
1. **Daily Streaks**: A visual grid/calendar that tracks consecutive days of platform activity.
2. **Note Taker**: A context-menu or selection-overlay that allows students to highlight AI-generated text and save it directly to a personal digital notebook.
3. **Badges**: Continued gamification tailored for academic persistence.

## Need (Rationale)
Middle school is a transition period where students need to develop organizational skills. Streak tracking encourages consistency, while the note-taker simplifies the synthesis of AI-provided information into study materials.
